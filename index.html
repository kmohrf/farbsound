farbsound

About

farbsound is an audio web player that takes its playlist from the site you visit

How?

Once you visit a site you start farbsound via a bookmarklet. It will load the tracks that are linked in the current page and allows you to play them directly in your browser.

Bookmarklet?

Exactly. Just drag & drop the logo from above into your bookmark bar and click on it once you visit a site that links to audio files.
